Paint It Black!

(Regular Text)

Paint It Black! is a Firefox extension with a very simple goal — it forces text to be black, whenever possible.

Bacon ipsum dolor sit amet meatloaf biltong venison shoulder spare ribs porchetta, rump sirloin filet mignon ribeye doner beef ribs.

(Gray Text)

Many sites use gray text on a bright white background. I could write a long rant about this, but Contrast Rebellion says it better, more completely, and more politely than I can, and gives useful pointers to further reading.

Filet mignon ground round t-bone, pork chop fatback tri-tip flank capicola tail pancetta ribeye doner sausage meatloaf. Drumstick short ribs sirloin sausage shankle. Pastrami meatball corned beef, pig salami.

(Narrow Font Weight)

Some sites specify a narrow font weight. Even when the text color is black, it can be difficult to read. Paint It Black! will force the font weight to normal, whenever possible.

Turducken fatback prosciutto short loin. Jerky ham pork loin jowl pancetta kevin doner venison shank cow tenderloin ground round frankfurter.

(Dark Background)

Paint It Black! will not affect any text that is lighter than the background color. It may, however, force narrow fonts to normal weight.
